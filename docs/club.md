# Applied AI Club at Penn State

## What We Are

The Applied AI Club is a student organization at Penn State dedicated to helping students understand how AI is changing the way businesses operate. Our membership is primarily based in the Smeal College of Business, with students across accounting, finance, marketing, supply chain, MIS, risk management, and management. We welcome students from all colleges and majors.

Most of our members are still early in their exposure to how AI and technology are used in professional settings. Everything we do is built around closing that gap.

## What We Do

We run three kinds of programming.

**Guest speaker sessions and workshops.** We bring in professionals from all fields who can talk honestly about how AI has changed the way they work. Sessions are 30 to 45 minutes, Zoom or in person, followed by Q&A. We also run hands-on workshops and case competitions where members work through real business problems using AI. Open to all Penn State students.

**Labs.** For members who want to go deeper. Labs takes on full application builds using the same tools and workflows used in professional engineering teams. You pick a project, form a team, and ship it. The current stack includes Claude Code, Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, GitHub Actions, and Playwright.

**Tool registry.** We maintain a list of AI tools worth knowing, organized for students at every level. Each tool includes what it does, who makes it, and what you can do with it. Descriptions are written by club members. The registry currently includes tools across five categories: AI assistants, research tools, developer tools, creative tools, and code editors.

## Where We Are

We are in our pilot semester, Spring 2026. Formal meetings begin Fall 2026 at Penn State University Park. We are currently scheduling our first guest speakers and building out the site, workspace, and Labs infrastructure.

The club was recognized as a student organization through Penn State Student Affairs. We follow Penn State branding guidelines and the rules that apply to university-recognized student organizations.

## How to Get Involved

Students can join through the mailing list on our website or through our GroupMe. There is no application process. Membership is open to all Penn State students.

Professionals interested in speaking can sign up at appliedaipennstate.com/speakers or email appliedaipsu@gmail.com.
