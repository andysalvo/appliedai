# Guest Speaker Program

> Applied AI Club at Penn State
> v1

## What We Are

The Applied AI Club is a group of students at Penn State, mostly in the Smeal College of Business, who are exploring how AI is changing the workforce. We are business students seeking to understand where AI fits into our future careers.

We have three main initiatives:

1. **Events and Programming** -- We invite professionals who use AI in their work to share their experiences. Our goal is to have honest conversations about the practical applications of AI, rather than theoretical discussions or product demos.
2. **Applied AI Labs** -- A smaller group of members work on building real projects using AI tools like Claude, Next.js, and GitHub. This is an opportunity for members to gain hands-on experience.
3. **Explore AI** -- We maintain a list of AI tools and resources that we find useful, organized to help members discover new tools regardless of their level of experience.

## What We Are Looking For in a Speaker

We are interested in hearing from professionals who have experience working with AI and are willing to share their insights with our members.

To be considered as a speaker, you should:

- Have experience working with AI in a professional setting
- Be able to explain your work in a way that is easy for college students to understand
- Be willing to share your honest experiences, including challenges and lessons learned

**What we ask:**

- 30 to 45 minutes of your time, including Q&A
- Flexibility to present via Zoom or in person at Penn State University Park
- A brief description of your talk so we can inform our members about what to expect

**What you get:**

- An engaged audience of students who are genuinely interested in learning about AI
- The opportunity to connect with the next generation of business professionals who are interested in AI
- A recorded session, if you would like, for your own use

Please note that while we cannot offer monetary compensation, we believe that sharing your experiences can provide valuable insights to our members and contribute to the growth of the AI community.

We acknowledge that our program is still developing, and we are open to feedback and suggestions on how to improve. If you are interested in speaking or have ideas for our program, please do not hesitate to reach out.

We are committed to transparency and honesty in our program. While we strive to provide valuable experiences for both our speakers and members, we recognize that our program may not be perfect. We are continuously working to address our limitations and improve our initiatives.

By participating in our guest speaker program, you will be contributing to the education and growth of our members. We appreciate your consideration and look forward to the opportunity to work with you.
