### Secondary

### Rules

- Nittany Navy and Beaver Blue are the "primary brand blues." They must have strong presence in every design.
- **Never create solid tints** of the primary brand colors (no 70% Navy, no 50% Beaver Blue). Use opacity overlays on photos/video only.
- Beaver Blue at less than 100% appears purple. Always use it at full strength.
- Pugh Blue can serve as canvas or focus, but pair it with a primary brand blue somewhere.

## Color Formula: Canvas + Focus + Accent

Every design uses three layers:

1. **Canvas** (largest area, background): White Out, Pugh Blue, Nittany Navy, or Beaver Blue
2. **Focus** (headline, central element): If light canvas, use Nittany Navy or Beaver Blue. If dark canvas, use White Out or Pugh Blue.
3. **Accent** (smallest, highlights): Sparingly. Use accent colors from the palette below.

### Light Canvas (white/pugh blue background)

- Focus: Nittany Navy or Beaver Blue
- Accent: Any approved accent color, used sparingly

### Dark Canvas (navy/beaver blue background)

- Focus: White Out or Pugh Blue
- Accent: Any approved accent color, light/max-light versions

## Typography

The official Penn State fonts are not freely available. For web and digital:

- **Headings**: Use a clean serif or the project's display font
- **Body**: Use a clean sans-serif (Inter, system fonts)
- The brand book references proprietary fonts managed through Adobe CC Libraries

## For Student Organizations

Per Penn State Student Affairs:

- We **cannot use the Penn State logo** as our organization mark
- Our name format is "Applied AI Club **at Penn State**" (not "Penn State Applied AI Club")
- We can request the official University-Recognized Student Organization Shield Mark
- We can use: paw print graphic, Lion Shrine images (approved drawings), lion caricatures
- We **cannot use**: University Seal, Penn State building images, Athletics logos, Block "S"

## Applying This to Our Work

### Website (appliedaipennstate.com)

- Light canvas pages: White background, Nittany Navy headings, Beaver Blue buttons/links
- Dark canvas sections: Nittany Navy background, White text, Pugh Blue labels
- Gradient accent bar: Beaver Blue > PA Sky > Pugh Blue (our signature element)

### PDFs and Documents

- White canvas, Nittany Navy headings and rules
- Beaver Blue for links and emphasis
- No solid tints of Navy or Beaver Blue
- Clean, academic formatting

### AI Chat Widget

- Dark canvas (Beaver Blue glass) is acceptable as an accent element
- Text should be White Out on dark surfaces
